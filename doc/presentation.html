DB

member

컬럼비고
idPK
phoneUNIQUE
name, points, created_at

menu

컬럼비고
idPK
categoryFOOD / DRINK
price, image_url, available…

orders

컬럼비고
member_idFK
statusRECEIVED / PREPARING / COMPLETED / CANCELLED

coupon: code, discount_type, used, expires_at
admin: username, password(BCrypt)
